Olive Defence Entrance Academy

About Us

Olive Defence Entrance Academy is an online learning platform dedicated to helping students prepare for competitive defence examinations such as CDS, NDA, AFCAT, and other similar exams.

Our objective is to provide structured and exam-focused preparation through clear concept explanations, exam-oriented practice questions, and focused revision material designed specifically for defence entrance examinations.

Our Training Approach

At Olive Defence Entrance Academy, we focus on conceptual clarity and practical exam strategies. Our lessons are designed to help students:

  • Understand important concepts clearly
  • Practice exam-level MCQs regularly
  • Improve speed and accuracy in solving questions
  • Revise important topics efficiently before exams

The learning system includes structured theory lessons, quick revision sections, and quiz practice so that students can systematically strengthen their preparation for defence entrance examinations.

Trainer

The academy is led by competitive exam training experts with over 10 years of experience in designing training courses and preparing students for various competitive examinations.

The training approach focuses on clear concept explanation, structured study methods, and exam-oriented practice to help students perform confidently in defence entrance examinations.
